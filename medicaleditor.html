Medical Editor

The ideal candidate will be experienced Medical Editor. Editing copy decks, client blogs, and have a very strong understanding of writing for the web and SEO best practices. Candidate must be an excellent writer, very deadline oriented and able to work in a fast-paced environment.

Duties & Responsibilities
  • Proofread, re-structure and edit blogs by content writers
  • Optimize published pieces using SEO guidelines
  • Edit all copy for both language and subject/content accuracy
  • Ensure best practices and style guides are followed for all copy writing
  • Provide construction feedback to writers to help them improve their writing
  • Ensure all deadlines are achieved
  • Understand all client medical specialties and have a vast knowledge of all medical fields
  • Edit copy decks produced by other writers for grammar, punctuation and spelling
  • Ability to edit several blogs per week and meet deadlines
Requirements
  • 2+ years of solid experience writing medical copy
  • 2+ years of experience editing blogs for SEO
  • Excellent attention to detail
  • Excellent writing, editing, proofreading skills with a demonstrable portfolio of published work
  • Understanding of SEO best practices
  • Understanding of medical conditions and terminology and how to explain things in lay-friendly terms
  • Must be very comfortable and proficient using webmail or Outlook
  • Must have experience using MS Word
  • Knowledge of WordPress preferred
  • We are 100% paperless so candidate must be very comfortable working in a variety of online systems
  • Must be a multi-tasker and be able to juggle multiple projects at once
