Quality Assurance Specialist

The Quality Assurance Specialist ensures that client websites/projects are free from errors and defects through extensive testing. The QA Specialist will possess a high degree of technical aptitude and experience in web technologies. Front-End development experience is highly desirable. The QA Specialist will be someone who can effectively multitask and work with development teams.

Duties & Responsibilities
  • Review functional and design specifications to ensure understanding of project
  • Evaluate and confirm websites are pixel perfect in design, functioning as they should and meet the specifications and requirements
  • Conduct tests for browser and device compatibility
  • Test online forms
  • Review content/copy on all website pages and ensure accuracy
  • Identify and document bugs/errors and communicate them to developers
  • Validate proper code and plugins used by developers
  • Participate in review of standards, procedures, tools and process
  • Assist project leaders in solving quality assurance issues
  • Collaborate effectively with team members to solve problems in time
Requirements
  • 2+ years of relevant QA experience
  • Experience with software testing, including knowledge of testing phases and approaches
  • Experience working with desktop, mobile, and web applications
  • Experience defining bugs/defects
  • Successful experience in working with agencies on fast paced projects
  • Excellent attention to detail
  • Strong critical analysis skills, ability to think clearly and rationally, understanding the logical connection between ideas
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills in English
